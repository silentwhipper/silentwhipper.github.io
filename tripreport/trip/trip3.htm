2016.03.20 - Malibu Creek

Day trip to local crag with Min-feng and Linhan. I onsighted K2 (5.9) which was my first successful lead climb outdoor and then Born on the 4th of July (5.8). These are good warm up. Linhan was leading Born on the 4th of July and fell hit her head to the wall. Good thing she has her helmet on. I attempted Family Jewel (5.10c) on the Gorgeous Wall. The crux was on an difficult overhanging pocket. Got pretty pump and stuck there. I had to get lowered, took a rest, then went back to finish it. I still had to rest on draws but at least this time got to the anchor.
