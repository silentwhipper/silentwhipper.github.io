2016.04.16 - Joshua Tree

I visited Joshua Tree National Park for the first time to do some bouldering. We first headed to the Outback area. We then hopped on stuff on Dino's Egg boulder. I did Dino's Egg (V0 R) and Platypus (V0). Platypus has a scary top out which requires a pull on poor sloper. We made an attempt on Chuckawalla (V1), which was fun but I could not get the send.

Cool feature rock Wide crack of Hobbit Hole Offwidth (V0)

In the afternoon, we moved to explore Crystal Skull area. I managed to send an unnamed V2 on the east face of the crystal skull boulder.
