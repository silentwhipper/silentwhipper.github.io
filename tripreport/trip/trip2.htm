2016.03.05 - Stoney Point

I heard about this urban bouldering spot right in the middle of the city, so I went to check it out by myself. It was the first time for me at the area, so I was pretty lost. I got on Critter Crack (V0), which is the area classic. I climbed up about half way and hopped off due to not wanting to commit to a sketchy landing. Then a group of boulderers showed up and helped with the spotting. I then could commit and sent the problem. I also gave Pancake (V2) a few tries but could not send it yet.
